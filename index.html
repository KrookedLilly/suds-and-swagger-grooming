Meet Your Groomer

Angel Lyons

With 20+ years of professional grooming experience, Angel has seen it all — and groomed it all. From tiny teacup breeds to 100-pound German Shepherds, every pet gets the same careful, personalized attention.

Angel is AKC S.A.F.E. Grooming certified, meaning your pet is in the hands of a groomer who meets the highest standards of safety, skill, and animal care in the industry.

But what truly sets Angel apart is the bond she builds with every pet and owner. Clients describe a family-like connection — the kind of trust that keeps them coming back year after year. Some clients who have moved away from Spokane Valley say they still haven't found anyone as good.

At Suds & Swagger, your pet isn't just another appointment. They're part of the family.
